About Bellevue Hotel

Bellevue Hotel is a five-star oceanfront retreat at Bellevue Cove. Since 1968, it has
stood at the edge of the cove as the coastline's quiet constant — built around
unhurried service and a view that hasn't needed to change in fifty years.

Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. The hotel is 25 minutes from
Bellevue Regional Airport. On-site amenities include the spa, an infinity pool, a
kids' club, and a fitness center.
