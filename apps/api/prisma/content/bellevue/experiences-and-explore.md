## Local Recommendations

- Harbor Row Sushi — dining, 10 minute drive. The hotel's own pick for a special dinner out.
- Cliffside Coastal Trail — outdoors, 5 minute walk. The best sunrise view in Bellevue Cove.
- The Vintage District — shopping, 15 minute drive. Boutique galleries and wine bars.
- Marina Farmers Market — Saturdays only. Guests love the fresh oyster stand.
