# Bellevue Hotel — Policies

| Topic | Rule |
| --- | --- |
| Check-in / check-out | Check-in from 3:00 PM, check-out by 11:00 AM. Early check-in is subject to availability and not guaranteed. |
| Pets | Pets are welcome in select rooms for a fee of $75 per night. Breed restrictions may apply. |
| Cancellation | Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival; one night is charged after that. |
| Smoking | The Bellevue is a non-smoking property. Designated outdoor areas only. A $250 cleaning fee applies if violated. |
| Parking | Valet parking is $45 per night. Self-parking is $30 per night. |
| Extra guest | A third guest in applicable room types is $50 per night. |
